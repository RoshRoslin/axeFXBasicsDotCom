About Me

Welcome to AxeFX Basics. My name is Rosh and I have been programming Fractal Products for over 10 years. My clients include: Def Leppard, Melissa Etheridge, A Perfect Circle, Steve Vai, Dweezil Zappa, Bush, and more.

I want to give back to the Fractal Community and show some approaches and techniques for programming all your Fractal Audio Systems Products.
