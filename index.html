About Me

Welcome to Axe FX Basics. My name is Rosh and I have been programming Fractal Products for over 10 years. My clients include: Def Leppard, Melissa Etheridge, A Perfect Circle, Steve Vai, Dweezil Zappa, Bush and more.

I wanted to give back to the Fractal Community and show some approaches and techniques for programming all your Fractal Audio Systems Products.
